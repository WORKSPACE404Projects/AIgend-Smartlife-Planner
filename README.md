# AIgend-Smartlife-Planner

🤖 AIgend: Your Sovereign Life Planner

AIgend is an open‑source, privacy‑first personal assistant that helps you coordinate your nutrition, fitness, finances, and calendar – all while keeping you in control of your data.

✨ Features

· Multi‑Coach Architecture
  · 🥗 Diet Coach – Personalized weekly meal plans based on your goals, preferences, and allergies.
  · 🏋️ Fitness Coach – Tailored workout routines adapted to your equipment, facilities, and any injuries.
  · 💰 Financial Coach – Smart advice to help you manage your budget, savings, and investments.
  · 📅 Daily Briefing – A proactive summary of your day, including weather, agenda, and personalised suggestions.
  · 🛒 Purchase Analyzer – Get an honest, context‑aware opinion before you spend money.
· Built for Sovereignty
  · Your data stays on your device (localStorage), with optional sync via your own cloud.
  · AI models can run locally (Ollama, WebLLM) or be self‑hosted – no vendor lock‑in.
· Cross‑Platform
  · Web app (Next.js) and native mobile apps via Capacitor (Android/iOS).
  · Works offline, respects your privacy.

🧠 Why AIgend?

AIgend is more than a tool – it’s a proof of concept for the ideas in Misery of Cryptography.
It demonstrates how distributed property and sovereign nodes can be built today, using real code that you can run, modify, and own.

We believe that the people who generate value – maintainers, makers, users – should collectively decide how that value is used. AIgend is our first step toward that future.
